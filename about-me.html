What I Love.

Hi, I’m Danila, a frontend developer with a passion for crafting websites that not only work but feel right.

For over four years, I’ve been immersed in the world of web development, turning ideas into functional, responsive, and visually engaging experiences. My expertise lies in HTML, CSS (SCSS), JavaScript (jQuery), and PHP for database interactions. Recently, I’ve been diving deeper into React, Redux, and TypeScript to expand my toolkit and take on modern development challenges.

What drives me? The thrill of solving problems creatively and the satisfaction of seeing my work come to life on a screen. I believe in writing clean, semantic code that’s easy to maintain and genuinely enjoyable to work with. I thrive on teamwork, version control (GitLab is my daily companion), and building solutions that make users’ lives easier.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me lost in music—it inspires and fuels my creativity.
